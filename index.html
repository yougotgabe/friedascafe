Our Story

Old School.
On Purpose.

"We like to keep things old school — retro look, cheap coffee, cheap food, and friendly service."

We're a small cafe in the heart of downtown Willmar. Family owned and operated, we've been feeding this community since 1970 — and we don't plan on changing much. Hearty breakfasts, homemade desserts, wholesome sandwiches, and coffee that won't break the bank.

Our menu is a nod to the good ol' days. Whether you're craving grandma's pie, a classic grilled cheese, or pancakes so big they hang off the plate — you'll find it here. Every dish is made with love and the finest ingredients.

— Steve and Amy
