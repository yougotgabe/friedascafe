Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Frieda's Cafe ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at friedascafe.com. This policy explains what information we collect when you shop for merchandise on our site, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Information We Collect

When you make a purchase, our payment processor Stripe collects your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. We do not store your credit card details — that information is handled entirely by Stripe and never touches our servers.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics software, or advertising pixels on this website.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information collected at checkout is used solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your order through our print-on-demand partner, Printify
  • Send you order confirmation and shipping notifications
  • Respond to questions or concerns about your order

Who We Share Your Information With

We share your order information with the following third-party services only as necessary to fulfill your purchase:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Printify — print-on-demand production and shipping (printify.com/privacy-policy)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Data Retention

Order records are retained by Stripe and Printify according to their own policies. We do not maintain a separate customer database.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to ask about the personal information associated with your order or to request its deletion. See the contact information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Refund Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

All merchandise sold through friedascafe.com is printed on demand specifically for each order. Because of this, we do not accept returns or exchanges for buyer's remorse, incorrect size selection, or change of mind.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or with a printing error, we will make it right. Please contact us within 30 days of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • A photo of the damaged or defective item

We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Lost or Undelivered Orders

If your order is lost in transit, contact us and we will work with Printify to investigate and resolve the issue, including sending a replacement if necessary.

Wrong Address

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not responsible for orders shipped to an incorrectly entered address. If you catch a mistake before the order ships, contact us immediately and we will do our best to correct it.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.
